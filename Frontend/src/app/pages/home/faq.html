Frequently Asked Questions

To register for an exam, go to the "Register" page, fill out the required information, and submit your details. You will receive a confirmation email once your registration is successful.

You can view the exam schedule on the "Upcoming Exams" page. The page provides details of upcoming exams, including dates, times, and subjects.

If you forget your password, go to the "Login" page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password.

You can access study materials by navigating to the "Study Materials" section. Here, you'll find resources organized by subjects and topics to help you prepare for your exams.

You can contact support by visiting the "Contact" page. Fill out the form with your query, and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.
